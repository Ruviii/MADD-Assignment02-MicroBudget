## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

### 1. **Simplicity Over Complexity**
Every feature serves a clear purpose. No bloat, no confusion, just essential budgeting tools.

### 2. **Visual Before Numerical**
Colors, icons, and charts make financial data instantly understandable without reading numbers.

### 3. **Proactive Not Reactive**
ML predictions help you make decisions before money leaves your wallet, not just track where it went.

### 4. **Opinionated Defaults**
The envelope budgeting method is proven to work. We make it the core experience without compromising flexibility.
